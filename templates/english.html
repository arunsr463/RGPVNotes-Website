UNIT 1:

Grammar:

Applied Grammar and usage, Parts of Speech, Articles, Tenses,

UNIT 2:

Communication:

Introduction, Meaning and Significance, Process of Communication,
Oral and Written Communication, 7 c’s of Communication, Barriers to Communication
and Ways to overcome them, Importance of Communication for
Technical students.
UNIT 3:

Nonverbal Communication and listening skills:>

nonverbal communication, cross
cultural communication, communicating through visuals, listening, types of listening,

UNIT 4:

Developing Writing Skills:

Planning, Drafting and Editing, Precise Writing,
Précis, Technical definition and Technical description. Report Writing: Features of writing
a good Report, Structure of a Formal Report, Report of Trouble,
Laboratory Report, Progress Report.
UNIT 5:
Business Correspondence:

Importance of Business Letters, Parts and Layout;
Application, Contents of good Resume, guidelines for writing Resume, Calling/ Sending Quotation,
Order, Complaint, E-mail and Tender.
