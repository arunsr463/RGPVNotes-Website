ABOUT OUR WEBSITE

This website is all about helping students who are in colleges . What made me to create this website is that when I was in my college I faced many problems during exam day's about searching important notes , youTube videos , previous years question , special notes of college teachers . Some of these things I was getting but it was very hard for me to search different websites for different contents which are related to the same subject.

This reason was enough for me to create this website . In this website I collected all the important information related to a subject at a single page . Like one one can get previous year papers, related youTube videos, special notes of teachers, standard notes .
